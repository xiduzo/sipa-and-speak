## Product Purpose

Sip&Speak exists to turn the language gap between TU/e's international and Dutch student communities into a mutual advantage. International students arriving in Eindhoven want to practice Dutch and connect with local life. Dutch students want to improve their skills in the languages those internationals speak. The app pairs them by language goals and shared interests, then arranges in-person "S&S moments" at local Eindhoven venues. It is not a chat platform or a classroom — it is the infrastructure that makes real-world, peer-to-peer language exchange inevitable.

## Target Users

| Actor | Context | Primary goals |
| --- | --- | --- |
| International student | Newly arrived in Eindhoven, enrolled at or alumni of TU/e; speaks a language a Dutch peer wants to learn | Practice Dutch in a low-pressure setting; connect with local students; feel at home in the city |
| Dutch student / alumnus | Enrolled at or graduated from TU/e; wants to improve in a language an international peer speaks natively | Find a conversation partner around a shared interest; build a sustainable practice habit |
| Venue partner | Local Eindhoven café or retailer hosting S&S meetups | Receive student foot traffic; get engagement reports from the platform |

## Core Principles

1. **Real-world first** — The app is infrastructure, not the destination. Every feature decision should ask: does this make an in-person S&S moment more likely?
2. **Safety by design** — Messaging is unlocked only after a meetup has taken place. TU/e email verification keeps the community trusted and bounded.
3. **Simplicity & low friction** — 95% of new users complete their profile in under 20 minutes. Core flows (matching, scheduling, meeting) must be learnable in ≤5 minutes.
4. **Privacy & compliance** — Data collection is minimal, retention is automated, and the platform meets GDPR requirements by default — not as an afterthought.

## Strategic Goals

- [ ] Reach 100+ concurrent active users within the TU/e community with venue partnerships established at ≥3 Eindhoven locations
- [ ] 90% of matched student pairs schedule a meetup within 3 weeks of being connected
- [ ] Earn official endorsement from the TU/e international office and integration into the student onboarding programme, with measurable improvement in integration survey scores

## Bounded Contexts

| Identity & Onboarding | TU/e-scoped registration via institutional email; alumni verified against a separate registered email database; language profile and interest setup | `user`, `languageProfile`, `userLanguage`, `userInterest` |
| Matching | Pairing students by language compatibility and shared interests | `matchRequest`, user progression states (Registering → Matched) |
| Meetup Scheduling | Proposing, negotiating, and confirming S&S moments at venues | `meetup`, `venue`, availability slots |
| Conversation | Post-meetup messaging between matched pairs | `conversation`, `message`, `messageReadStatus` |
| Trust & Moderation | Flagging, review, and removal of disruptive participants | flag records, moderator actions, user state transitions |

## Out of Scope

- **General social networking** — Sip&Speak is not a friendship or dating app; relationships beyond language exchange are not a design target
- **Non-TU/e users** — Open public registration outside the TU/e community (students and registered alumni) is not supported
- **Online-only meetups** — Virtual language sessions (video calls, etc.) are not a use case; the product requires physical presence
- **Formal language instruction** — No structured lessons, curriculum, assessments, or teacher roles
